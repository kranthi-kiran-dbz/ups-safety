UPS, Battery & Network Server Room

Safety Information

⚠️ Why This Room Is Restricted

This room contains UPS systems, inverter batteries, and critical network equipment essential for power backup and internet connectivity.

⚠️ Health & Safety Risks

1. Battery Gas (Hydrogen)

  • Batteries release hydrogen gas during charging
  • This gas is invisible and flammable
  • Poor ventilation can make air unsafe

2. AC Does NOT Make It Safe

  • AC only cools air, it does not remove gases
  • Closed AC rooms can still be dangerous

3. Electric Shock Risk

  • High voltage equipment present
  • Touching can cause serious injury

4. Air Quality

  • Room may feel cool but air may not be safe

🚫 Important Rules

  • Do not enter without permission
  • Do not sit or rest inside
  • Do not touch any equipment

✅ Safety Measures

  • Proper ventilation systems
  • Restricted access
  • Maintenance by trained staff
